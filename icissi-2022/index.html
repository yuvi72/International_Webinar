Welcome To ICISSI-2022

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to join ICISSI-2022 (International Conference On Intelligent Systems and Smart Infrastructure ( ICISSI 2022) to be held at one of the most ancient and holy cities of India "PRAYAGRAJ”, Uttar Pradesh from 21th & 22st April 2022. The conference is being jointly organised by Shambhunath Institute of Engineerinng and Technology PRAYAGRAJ, Institute of Engineering and Technology, Lucknow, and Manipal University Jaipur with an aim to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians and engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new challenges being faced in the field of emerging technologies. This conference will be a platform to focus on the core technological developments in the emerging fields like machine learning, intelligence systems, smart infrastructure, advanced power technology etc.

Proposed Publishing & Indexing Information: All accepted & presented papers of the conference by duly registered authors will be submitted to Springer book Series “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering (LNEE)” (https://www.springer.com/series/7818) and indexed in EI Compendex and Scopus databases.
