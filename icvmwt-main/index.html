    • Welcome To ICVMWT-2021

    It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to join ICISSI-2022 (International Conference On Intelligent Systems and Smart Infrastructure ( ICISSI 2022) to be held at one of the most ancient and holy cities of India "PRAYAGRAJ”, Uttar Pradesh from 21th & 22st April 2022. The conference is being jointly organised by SIET PRAYAGRAJ and IET Lucknow.

    , J.K. Institute of Applied Physics and Technology, University of Allahabad and Manipal University Jaipur with an aim to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians and engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new challenges being faced in the field of Electronics Engineering. This Conference will be a platform to focus on the core technological developments in the emerging fields like wireless communication, RF microwave/Radar, VLSI, Optical Communication and so on.

    Publishing & Indexing Information: All accepted & presented papers of the conference by duly registered authors will be submitted to Springer book Series “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering (LNEE)” (https://www.springer.com/series/7818) and indexed in EI Compendex and Scopus databases.
